Royal Enfield is an indian multinational motorcycle manufacturing comapny headquartered Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The royal enfield brand,including its original English heritage, is the oldest global motorcycle brand is contineous production. The first Royal Enfield motorcycle was built in 1901.The company makes classic-looking motorcycles including the Royal Enfield Bullet, Classic 350, Royal Enfield Thunderbird, Meteor 350, Classic 500, Interceptor 650, Continental and many more. Royal Enfield also make adventurous and offroading motorcycles like Royal Enfield Himalayan. Their motorcycles are equipped with single-cylinder and twin-cylinder engines.
